About The KKK

All the way from Limpopo in the deep tropics of Tzaneen in Nkowa-Nkowa. A kingdom of young hustlers striving to succeed through the streets.
Where it all started as a thought to sell Kotas, a year later, we're still kicking, serving the people of Nkowa-Nkowa.
It's not what we envisioned, yet it came with better lessons.
Quit, start, and grow; so far, we're still on the right track.
Food and friends, life and love, the bond that binds us together.
We hope that we serve you well as kings serve their kingdoms.
It's KKK Till Forever.
